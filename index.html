League of Legends

Also known as League and LoL (lawl)

Created by Riot Games and is rated T for Teen. It contains blood, fantasy violence, mild suggestive themes, and use of alcohol and tabaco.

About

League is an multiplayer online battle arena game, usually refered to as a MOBA. It's essencially a game of capture the flag. Official site and information found at leagueoflegends.com

How To Play

The game is played in matches. In the game you have. But First off in order to play you press the button at the top of the League of Legends client that says "Play". After that thare are several game modes and a variety on some of them. Once you have selected a game mode you choose your champion you load up into what ever map you corresponds with the game mode. Each champion has four abilities keybound to Q, W, E, and R keys. Q, W, and E usually have 5 levels and R usually only has 3. You can only upgrade a single abilty per level. In every match you play you will always start at just level 1 and killing enemies monsters or destroying enemy structures will give you xp which will get you level ups if you get enough of. The goal of the game is to destroy turrets and enemy champions in order to get to the core of the enemy base and destroy it.

Gaming, Fun and Rewarding

League of Legends is considered an ESport. Probably the number one game in ESports. Riot and LoL ESports host competitions for the game all over the world. there is even a World Championship event. In schools ESports are getting big too. Serious enough that schools are offering scholarships to the most skilled in League of Legends of the community to form a team and someday get into the League Championships of their region.
