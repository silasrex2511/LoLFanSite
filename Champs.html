Champions

Roles

Top

The top laner is usually the tank of the team. The tanks have most of the armor and most of the resists. These top laners usually stand in the front line and take up as much damage as possible from enemies in order to defend the back line.


Usual character stat scaling for top laning include the following

  • Armor
  • Health
  • Magic Resist

Jungle

Junglers Are designated to no lane and are required to take advantage of shrubs and jungle monster buffs to keep up with ally and enemy laners. Jungle monsters are tougher and stronger than normal lane minions however they do give a juicier reward. After achieving certain tasks expected of them in the jungle they are expected to roam into lanes and assist allies in battle by flanking enemy laners strategically according to enemy mispositionings.


Usual character stat scaling for junglers include the following

  • Life Steal
  • Armor
  • Attack Damage
  • Attack speed

Mid

The mid laner is known for having the most burst damage of the team. These guys usually fall in the backline along with the bottom laner and as the tanks block enemy fire in the front line the mid laners are expected to dish out significant damage from a safe distance. Mid laners are also very relient on their abilities.


Usual character stat scaling for mid laning include the following

  • Cooldown reduction
  • Mana Regeneration
  • Ability Power

Bottom

Bottom laners aka an ADC (attack damage carry) tends to be the carrier of the most damage of the team. They are also called marksman and are known for being long ranged fighters. basically only auto attack instead of relying on abilities like the mid laner however both stand in the backline. Most ADCs are actually expected to be toxic to their supports and be excellent kill stealers which ADCs enterprate as kill SECURING


Usual character stat scaling for bottom laning include the following

  • Attack Damage
  • Attack Speed
  • Critical Chance
  • Life Steal(optional)

Support

Supports are assigned to the bottom lane and are required to assist their ADC with anything they need. Simple tasks like healing, shielding, buffing, providing vision, and debuffing enemies are ways a support becomes a support. typically rely on abilities to be useful are not expected to get kills or be great in 1v1 combat against anything but the enemy support


Usual character stat scaling for supporting include the following

  • Cooldown Reduction
  • Armor(optional)
  • Mana Regeneration
