Co-op Vs Ai

Co-op Vs Ai, is game against artificial intelligence, commonly known as "Bots". Many new players practice their champions on there. Experienced players try new champs and for FWOD [First Win Of the Day]. This game mode is commonly reffered to as "Bots". It has a lot less syllables than Co-op vs AI. There is three variations of this game mode. The variations are the level of difficulty starting at Intro, next level would be Beginner, and lastly Intermediate.
